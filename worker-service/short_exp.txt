User clicks Deposit / Withdraw
        ↓
savings-service creates GoalOperation with PENDING status
        ↓
savings-service publishes Kafka event: goal.operation.created
        ↓
worker-service consumes this event
        ↓
worker-service changes status:
PENDING → PROCESSING → CONFIRMED / FAILED
        ↓
worker-service updates goal.current_amount
        ↓
worker-service creates matching transaction in transaction-service
